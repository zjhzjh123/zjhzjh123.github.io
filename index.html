Jiaheng Zhang

I am a fourth-year Ph.D. candidate in Computer Science at UC Berkeley, where I am very fortunate to be advised by Prof. Dawn Song. I also work closely with Prof. Yupeng Zhang. And I am a member of RISE Lab, Initiative for Cryptocurrencies & Contracts Lab (IC3) and Berkeley AI Research (BAIR).

My research interests lie in computer security and cryptography, especially zero-knowledge proofs and their applications on blockchain and machine learning models.

Prior to coming to Berkeley, I received my Bachelor's degree in ACM Honors Class of Shanghai Jiao Tong University, where I worked under the supervision of Prof. Xiaotie Deng. During my undergraduate, I was also a research intern at Cornell, advised by Prof. Elaine Shi.

I am highly honored to receive the Facebook Fellowship in 2021.
